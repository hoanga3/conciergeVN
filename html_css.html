OUR STORY

conciergeVIETNAM is an international consulting and brokerage company with offices in the Vietnam (Hanoi) and the U.S. to maximize market space in South East Asia for client assets within the developing sectors of manufacturing, education, entertainment, and technology.
What started as a series of weeklong meetings in Hanoi early 2019 regarding international students and U.S. boarding schools, quickly transformed into an entrepreneurial concept to engage with U.S. investors and companies via lucrative economic opportunities in Vietnam and surrounding South East Asian countries. Welcome to conciergeVIETNAM……

WHAT WE DO:

It’s all about relationships.

conciergeVIETNAM strives to participate in the extraordinary multi-year Vietnam and neighboring South-East Asian countries’ growth story by combining international expertise and experience with local knowledge and execution ability. The conciergeVIETNAM team is committed to delivering excellence using an efficient and transparent investment process to create a rewarding experience for our investors. We identify opportunities that will create and capture value for our investors and companies while mitigating the uncertainties of a frontier market. We have a particular focus and expertise in manufacturing, education, real estate, and information technology but remain opportunistic on sectors and investment opportunities.
